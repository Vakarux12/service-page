How it works

Simple from start
to done

Three steps to get anything sorted — or to start earning by helping people around you.

1
📋

Post or Browse

Need help? Post a job request with your budget. Offering skills? Create a listing for what you do best. Either way, you're seconds from the right person.

2
💬

Chat & Agree

Message directly in the app. Send a formal price offer with a set amount and deadline. Both sides agree before anything moves forward.

3
✅

Get It Done

Payment is held in secure escrow until the job is complete and confirmed. Leave a review. Money releases instantly to the provider.

Built different

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

💬

Real-time chat with price offers

Negotiate naturally through conversation. When ready, send a formal price offer — amount, payment method, and deadline in one clean card. Accept or decline with a tap.

🔒

Secure escrow payments

Pay through Stripe. Funds are held securely until the job is confirmed complete — protecting both sides every time.

🛡️

Dispute resolution

Something didn't go as planned? Open a dispute with evidence. A moderator reviews both sides and issues a fair decision.

⭐

Verified reviews & ratings

Only real bookings generate reviews. Build your reputation or choose providers with confidence based on genuine feedback.

💰

Built-in wallet

Earn directly into your Verko wallet. Cash out to your bank via Stripe whenever you're ready. Minimum €10.
